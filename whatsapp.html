WhatsApp Dark Mode

As an avid user of the popular messaging app, WhatsApp, I (along with many others on Instagram) have always wanted a dark mode for this social media platform.

Design Rationale

Currently, the WhatsApp team is trying to release a dark mode for the app, but I wanted to make my own version anyway. This was modified from WhatsApp templates found online. I decided to use more matte colours as it is easier on the eyes, and having the alternating grey and navy makes message threads easier to distinguish.
